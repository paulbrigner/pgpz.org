What is Coming

The PGPZ Coalition will be a smaller, more action-oriented group within the broader PGPZ ecosystem, designed for people directly engaged in policy influence and policymaker engagement.

Policy Professional Network

A focused space for policy practitioners, advocates, and subject-matter contributors who are actively working on Zcash and privacy-preserving digital cash policy.

Strategic Coordination

A venue for aligning policymaker education, advocacy priorities, policy materials, and engagement plans across trusted participants.

Practical Engagement

Support for coordinated outreach that clearly explains Zcash, privacy-preserving compliance, and the public-interest case for digital cash.
