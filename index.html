Financial Privacy

Privacy keeps digital finance safe, practical, and trustworthy.

Financial privacy helps people and businesses protect themselves from theft, harassment, discrimination, and unwanted surveillance.

As financial services become more digital and global, banks, payment companies, platforms, and third parties collect and analyze far more transaction data. A single exposed data trail can reveal spending patterns, income, location, and relationships at scale.

A donor may want to support a political cause without public exposure.
A patient may need sensitive medical supplies without wide visibility.
A business may need to keep vendor payments and payroll confidential.

About PGPZ

Pretty Good Policy for Zcash.

PGP* for Zcash, or PGPZ, is focused on supporting the broader adoption of Zcash.

PGPZ educates stakeholders, makes Zcash more understandable and accessible in everyday settings, and promotes policy that supports Zcash ecosystem development and growth.

Why It Matters

Zcash has an important role to play in the digital asset ecosystem because it offers a practical path toward privacy, accessibility, and global use.

PGPZ creates a place for thoughtful participation, collaboration, and steady community leadership.

About Zcash

A cryptocurrency built for optional privacy.

Zcash is a cryptocurrency and blockchain built to let people send value with strong privacy protections. Like Bitcoin, it has a fixed supply and a decentralized network, but it uses zero-knowledge cryptography so shielded transactions can be verified without publicly revealing the sender, recipient, or amount.

Fixed supply and decentralized network.
Zero-knowledge cryptography for shielded transfers.
Selective disclosure with public-chain auditability.
